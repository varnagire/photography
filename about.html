I am a freelance photographer specialising in travel and street photography. I discovered my passion for photography whilst travelling and love to photograph different landscapes and different cultures. I like photographing people best of all, because their personalities can really shine through in the photographs. My portfolio secton includes examples of my work from destinations in Europe, Asia, South America, North and East Africa, the Indian subcontinental and the Middle East. The photographs you can see in my portfolio represent my favourite work from the various different places I've photographed. I live in Lithuania, but I am available for assignments worldwide.

If you would like to know more about image licensing, pricing or booking a personal photoshoot, do not hesitate to contact me!
